Problem Statement
Context:

You are developing a ride-sharing application that must support multiple payment methods such as credit card and PayPal.
The application should be easily testable, maintainable, and scalable to accommodate future enhancements
and new payment methods.

Requirements:

1.  Payment Processing:
    -   Support multiple payment methods (e.g., credit card, PayPal).
    -   New payment methods should be easily integrable without changing the core logic of the application.

2.  Testability:
    -   The system should be easily testable with the ability to mock dependencies.

3.  Maintainability and Scalability:
    -   The architecture should allow for future enhancements with minimal code changes.
